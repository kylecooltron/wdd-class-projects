Design Principles Document

Kyle Coulon

Alignment

Stack Overflow

stackoverflow.com

This mobile site exemplifies the alignment principle. Everything is centered nicely. The layout of the page navigation is simple, and there are minimal buttons crowding the screen. The page information is seperated into equal sections making it easier to understand and see. Nothing is cut off the screen or crowding other elements.

Visual Hierarchy

Steam

store.steampowered.com

I think this page displays visual hierarchy because it's pretty obvious what they want you to click on. The text inside the image links is much larger than anything else. The search is a little bit larger than the menu items, and that's probably because they know when people search for a game they are more likely to purchase something.

White Space and Clean Design

Epic Games

store.epicgames.com

I really like the way they have these image boxes that you can swipe through one at a time, it really cleans up the page. There is plenty of space, and that makes you want to scroll and see what's below. Nothing is too crowded, or hard to read. The nav bar and search button are also really spaced out and easy to click.

Site Purpose

The Jedidiah Redwoods State Park and Smith River area in Northern California has a number of unique businesses and opportunities for commerce. This website provides a landing page for people looking to explore the area, and get a larger-picture view of what local shops and businesses have to offer. On the commerce website, businesses can work together to announce and hosting community events, or coordinate discount packages and coupons that will enrich the growth of each business. The purpose is to help local businesses thrive, and make it easier for visitors and locals to engage in the local marketplace.

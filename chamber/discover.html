Waterfalls

Smith River is home to over twenty different waterfalls depending on the recent rainfall. These crystal clear waterfalls make for amazing hiking destinations and picture spots.

Steelhead Fishing

Local guide services provide life changing Steelhead fishing experiences to over 20,000 visitors a year! Smith River is home to 10,000 steelhead that have been surveyed migrating up it's various tributaries.

Steelhead Fishing Youtube Video

Beaches

Smith River beaches are known for their shells! Also water temperatures often deter many of California's usual water sports, the local beaches and tidepools are known for their beauty and their abundance of shells. There are two piers and hundreds of local shops and restraunts that overlook the beautiful crescent bay.

Red Pine Dam

Red Pine Dam is one of the largest Dam's in the state, providing electricity for two county's. The Red Pine Lodge is also located overlooking the breathtaking gorge and is a great place to stay that provides access to the Snowden Ski resort and Smith River fisheries.

Snow Sports

Norcal Outback is one of our premier tour guides for various snowsports in the region. From backcountry skiing to snowmobiling and extreme sledding, they provide equiptment and adventure packages for all ages. The Snowden Ski resort is also located nearby.
